# Dida Pricing

Dida is free for personal use and for small organizations. Larger
organizations need a subscription per user. Subscriptions are handled at
<https://code.didabit.com> (payment via Revolut Business).

| Tier | Who | Price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Personal | Individuals acting on their own behalf | Free |
| Small organization | Annual gross revenue under USD 100,000 | Free |
| Business | Annual gross revenue USD 100,000 – 1,000,000 | USD 5 per user/month, or USD 100 per user/year — minimum 2 seats |
| Enterprise | Annual gross revenue over USD 1,000,000 | USD 5 per user/month — minimum 10 seats |

Notes:

- Revenue includes affiliates, measured over the organization's last fiscal
  year.
- A "user" is a person who runs Dida in any month of the subscription period.
- Prices exclude VAT/sales tax where applicable.

  the license controls if the two disagree.
